Resume Requirements

To get accurate analysis, please ensure your resume contains the following sections:

  • 👤 Personal contact information (Name, Email, Phone)
  • 🎓 Education details (Degrees, Institutions, Graduation dates)
  • 💼 Work experience (Companies, Roles, Responsibilities, Duration, Achievements)
  • 🛠️ Skills section (Technical skills, Software proficiency, Programming languages)
  • 🏆 Certifications and Training (Professional certifications, Courses)
  • 🚀 Projects (Personal or professional projects with brief descriptions)
  • 🌍 Languages (Languages you speak and proficiency levels)
Note: Our enhanced parser can now extract up to 15 skills, 30 certifications, and 20 projects for more comprehensive analysis.
